  • Rooms can only be used by guests who are duly registered at the reception unless approved by the host.
  • We serve aqua guard filtered drinking water. Packaged drinking water is available on payment.
  • Please respect quiet hours between 10:00 PM & 7:00 AM to ensure everyone’s comfort.
  • The homestay bears no responsibility for any damage or loss of your personal belongings.
  • Guests are not allowed to take weapons, inflammable materials or other dangerous chemicals inside the homestay.
  • Gambling,contraband, prostitution and animals are strictly prohibited in the homestay premise
  • Guests are not allowed to take out from the Homestay room inventory (pillows, blankets, towels, electric kettle, etc).
  • For any damage caused by the guests to the homestay property, the guests are due to cover the cost of damage to the Homestay.

  • You are welcome to give us suggestions (if any) to serve you better and make your stay a comfortable one.
